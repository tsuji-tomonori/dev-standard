# Approval roles

- `requester`: confirms the request, acceptance, and final user outcome.
- `product-owner`: confirms value, scope, priority, and requirement tradeoffs.
- `architect`: confirms architecture and detailed design decisions.
- `security-owner`: confirms threat, privacy, security, and high-impact exception decisions.
- `qa-owner`: confirms test strategy, coverage, results, and quality risk.
- `operations-owner`: confirms operability, support, recovery, and maintenance readiness.
- `release-owner`: makes the operational Go/No-Go decision.
- `governance-owner`: approves process/skill improvements and final retrospective closure.

One person may hold multiple roles when organizational policy permits, but each role requires a separate record and comment. Agents may prepare the command; only the real role holder supplies the decision.
